My Birthday Trip to Southern Utah



For my birthay this year, Robert took me to Southern Utah. We drove down to Moab and stayed in Greenwell Inn. Saturday, February 22 we went and hiked to Delicate Arch. The weather was perfect and it was not too hot to be on a hike. We started our hike about 130pm. Each stage of the hike was amazing. We made it all the way up to the arch itself. We definitely got some beautiful pictures.

The next day we went back to Arches National Park and hiked into Landscape Arch. That hike was a much easier hike for us and took a lot less time. When we were done we drove through the park and visited many more quick stops to make good use of our pass for the park.

That night we drove over to Dead Horse Point to watch the sunset. Those photos were beautiful as well. To end the weekend we stopped by Goblin valley on our way home Monday Morning. This was a great weekend getaway, out in nature and away from the world.
